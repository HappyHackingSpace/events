Garage Meetup is back. This one's a Night Shift.

You know the vibe: an old garage, dim lights, too much caffeine, and a handful of people who'd rather be building something than doing literally anything else. That's us this Saturday.

We start in the evening. Ideas hit the table, we figure out what's worth building, form our crews, and warm up together. Grab a snack, find your people, and settle in. Then, when the night gets quiet and the city goes to sleep, we flip into deep-work mode and just build until it's done. However long that takes. 🌙

New here? Perfect. You don't need to know anyone or come with a finished idea. Just show up, bring your curiosity, and you'll leave the night having shipped something with people who get it.

Can't make it in person? You're still one of us. Share an idea, open an issue, send a PR. The garage door's always open.

Happy Hacking!
