A free weekly AI workshop for kids in Diyarbakır, organized together by **Happy Hacking Space** and **Çocuk Çalışmaları Derneği (ÇocukÇA)**.

Hands-on, not a lecture. The kids explore how AI actually works by building and experimenting.

**Details**

- Every Sunday: **July 26 to August 30, 2026**
- **11:00 to 13:00**, every week
- **Sümer Park, Diyarbakır**
- Grades 5-6-7 and 8-9
- **Free.** No cost, no catch.
